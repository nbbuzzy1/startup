Our Mission

Matching felines that need a home with worthy owners.

At Purrfect Match, we all come to work every day because we want to solve the biggest problem in feline adoptions. To often, felines are simply given to new owners without due consideration that they belong together. That is why 30% of all feline adoptions result in the owners returning the felines. With Purrfect Match, you create a unique and thorough profile telling us about your background and your goals with your ideal feline companion. We do the rest and provide you five matches based on our proprietary patent-pending software.

Stop wasting time with antiquated methods. Let us find your Purrfect Match.
